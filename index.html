About Me

I'm a cybersecurity enthusiast focused on becoming a SOC Analyst. I’ve spent a lot of time in the trenches of TryHackMe and other CTF platforms, sharpening my skills in threat detection, log analysis, and incident response. I’m comfortable working with a wide range of tools—whether it’s Wireshark for packet analysis, Splunk for digging through logs, or using SIEMs to make sense of alerts. I really enjoy investigating how things go wrong (and why), and figuring out how to stop it from happening again. Right now, I’m building my portfolio with real-world inspired labs, ongoing learning, and projects that mimic the kind of work done in a Security Operations Center. I'm always up for a challenge and constantly exploring new ways to level up my skills.
